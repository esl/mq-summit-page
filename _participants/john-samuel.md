John Samuel is an experienced technologist and business founder of Seventh State technology company based out of the UK. His interest in computing started in the 80s and still maintains a child like interest in dismantling and putting together things whether that’s technical or business related.

Today technology is reshaping the world across many dimensions and he remains optimistic that this will be for the betterment of society.

When he’s not deep diving into the latest technologies, John enjoys soaking up culture and history on city breaks with his family.
