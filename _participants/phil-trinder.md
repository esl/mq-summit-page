Phil Trinder is a Professor of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow and a leading researcher in parallel and distributed
programming languages.  He has researched Erlang technologies for 20 years, including coordinating the very successful EU RELEASE project to improve the scaling of Erlang. Currently the team at Glasgow are exploring how to detect communication errors in BEAM languages.
