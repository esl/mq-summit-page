ABOUT THE EVENT

Be Part of the First MQ Summit!

Message queues are the backbone of modern distributed systems, ensuring seamless communication, scalability, and resilience.

Building on the success of RabbitMQ Summit, MQ Summit now brings together professionals working with a wide range of messaging systems, including RabbitMQ, Kafka, NATS, Apache Pulsar, Amazon SQS, IBM MQ, and more. Whether you're developing event-driven architectures, optimizing system performance, or making strategic technology decisions, this conference offers valuable insights from industry experts.

Why Attend?
A. Deep Dive into Messaging – Learn about RabbitMQ, Kafka, Pulsar, NATS, Amazon SQS, Google Pub/Sub, and more.
B. Technical & Strategic Insights – Whether you build with MQs or decide which to adopt, gain knowledge from experts.
C. Network with Industry Leaders – Connect with peers, technology creators, and business decision-makers.
D. Real-World Case Studies – See how messaging solutions power mission-critical applications.
